Our Team

The minds behind EMOS

Research Team

EMOS is developed by a dedicated team of researchers combining expertise in materials science, computational physics, and software engineering. This project originated as a research initiative from the APRIL AI Hub, with a vision to tackle critical challenges in materials discovery for electronics devices and applications. Our mission is to curate high-quality data and develop sophisticated AI models that enable efficient, reproducible, and scalable materials discovery. By creating a unified platform that integrates diverse databases, computational tools, and machine learning models, we aim to democratize access to advanced materials research capabilities and accelerate the development of next-generation electronic materials.
